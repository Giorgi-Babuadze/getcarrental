FAQ
What conditions must be met when renting a car?

Foreign citizens can rent a car with a passport and a driver's license. According to the laws of Azerbaijan, they have the right to drive a car in the country during the first month after entering.

Can foreigners rent a car?

Yes, foreigners can rent a car if all conditions are met.

Is a deposit required?

Usually, a deposit is required, but the conditions may vary.

What documents are needed to rent a car?

A passport, a driver's license, and a credit card are required.
